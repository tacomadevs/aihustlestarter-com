This Privacy Policy explains how AI Hustle Starter Pack ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you visit aihustlestarter.com, purchase our products, or sign up for our newsletter.

---

## What We Collect

**When you make a purchase**, we collect:

- Name and email address
- Shipping address (for physical workbook delivery)
- Payment information (processed by TikTok Shop or Stripe — we do not store card numbers)
- Order details

**When you sign up for our newsletter or submit a form**, we collect:

- Name and email address
- Phone number (if you provide it — optional)

**Automatically**, we collect:

- IP address and general location
- Browser type and device information
- Pages visited and time spent on site
- Referral source (how you found us)

This data is collected via Cloudflare (our hosting provider) and, once Google Analytics is active, via Google Analytics.

---

## How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

- Fulfill your order and ship your workbook
- Send your lesson access link and welcome email
- Deliver the newsletter and course content you purchased
- Send transactional emails related to your order or account
- Send marketing emails about new lessons, updates, and offers (you can unsubscribe at any time)
- Improve our website and content
- Comply with legal obligations

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

---

## Who We Share Your Information With

**Stripe** — our payment processor. When you purchase directly on this site, your payment details are handled by Stripe. We do not store card numbers. Stripe's privacy policy is available at [stripe.com](https://stripe.com/privacy).

**GoHighLevel (GHL)** — our CRM and email platform. Your name, email, and purchase tags are stored in GHL and used to send you emails and manage your account. GHL's privacy policy is available at [gohighlevel.com](https://www.gohighlevel.com).

**TikTok Shop** — if you purchase through TikTok Shop, your order and contact information is subject to TikTok's privacy policy in addition to ours.

**Shipping carriers** — your name and address are shared with the carrier to fulfill your workbook order.

**YouTube** — lesson videos are embedded from YouTube. YouTube may collect data when you watch a video. See [Google's privacy policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).

**Cloudflare** — our hosting and CDN provider processes traffic to our site.

**Legal requirements** — we may disclose your information if required by law or to protect our rights.

---

## Email Marketing

By purchasing or signing up, you agree to receive emails from us. Every marketing email includes an unsubscribe link. Transactional emails (order confirmations, lesson access) cannot be opted out of as they are required to fulfill your purchase.

We do not spam. We send content related to AI side hustles, new lessons, and product updates.

---

## Cookies

Our site uses cookies for:

- **Essential functions** — keeping the site running correctly
- **Analytics** — understanding how visitors use the site (once Google Analytics is active)
- **Cloudflare** — security and performance cookies set by our hosting provider

You can disable cookies in your browser settings. Some site features may not work correctly without them.

---

## Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account or subscription is active, and for up to 3 years after your last interaction with us, unless a longer period is required by law. You can request deletion at any time (see below).

---

## Your Rights

You have the right to:

- **Access** the personal data we hold about you
- **Correct** inaccurate information
- **Delete** your data (subject to legal obligations)
- **Opt out** of marketing emails at any time via the unsubscribe link
- **Request a copy** of your data in a portable format

California residents have additional rights under CCPA, including the right to know what personal information we collect and the right to opt out of the sale of personal information (we do not sell personal information).

To exercise any of these rights, contact us via the [Support page](/support).

---

## Children's Privacy

Our site is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 13. If you believe a child has submitted information to us, contact us and we will delete it promptly.
